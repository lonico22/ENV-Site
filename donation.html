MICA and the Environmental Design Department would like to thank you for taking an interest in our project and our efforts.
If you would like to donate in support of our efforts, please follow this link to the MICA Environmental Design Donations page.

Follow these instructions to ensure your donation goes towards our project:
· Click on the “Click here to contribute to the department” link.
· You will be redirected to the MICA Online Giving Page.
· Fill out the Donor Information fields as indicated.
· In the “MAKE A GIFT” portion of the online form, be sure to enter a Gift Amount.
· In the field “I would like my gift to support:” click “Restrict my gift to:” and another filed will pop up.
· Type in “ENV Design-Build Shelter Prototype Project”.
· Complete the online form and SUBMIT the form.

We thank you in advance for your contribution. Without your support, the project could not be realized.
